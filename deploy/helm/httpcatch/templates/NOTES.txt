Security notes:

* The admin port exposes /healthz and /metrics WITHOUT authentication. Anyone
  who can reach the admin Service (or the Ingress, if enabled) can scrape
  metrics and probe health. Restrict at the ingress layer (IP allowlists,
  auth middleware) if the admin host is reachable beyond your operators.

* PVC retention: whenDeleted={{ .Values.persistence.retentionPolicy.whenDeleted }}, whenScaled={{ .Values.persistence.retentionPolicy.whenScaled }}.

  during that window may be lost (proxies do not retry POSTs).
